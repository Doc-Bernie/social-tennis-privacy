Features

Everything your club needs

Built specifically for social tennis — no bloat, no complexity. Just the tools that matter.

🎾

Session Sign-Ups

Players join sessions in seconds. Automatic waitlist management promotes players when spots open up.

🏆

Automated Draw

Generate balanced court draws instantly. Mix partners between rounds, customise court names, and publish with one tap.

🔒

Club-Scoped Access

Each club gets its own private space. Players access their club via a unique code — completely separate from other clubs.

💳

Payment Tracking

Players mark themselves as paid. Admins confirm payments and track who's settled up — no spreadsheets needed.

Admin Notifications

Get notified when players join, leave, or submit payment. Stay on top of your session without checking constantly.

Terms & Conditions

Set custom terms for your club that all players can read. Update anytime and it syncs instantly across all devices.
